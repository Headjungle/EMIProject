HOW TO PLAY BUBBLECATCH (Version 0.9.8 Stable)

1. click Starten
2. click the bubbles in the right order(from smallest number to biggest number)
3. end

score: the sum of the values the bubbles are tagged with, wrong choices are
punished with a negative addition of the value the wrong bubble was tagged with
time: in minutes and seconds

highscore: highest points in the most fastest time
